A small look into my life

OPEN-MINDED. FEARLESS. CREATIVE. TEAM-ORIENTED.

Open-Minded. Change fuels our world and creates the future. Innovations excite me. I love learning about scientific and technological developments, trying them out, and thinking about what impacts they will have on global issues, our relationships with each other and the world, and the trajectory of our planet.

Fearless. I am not afraid of a challenge. I taught myself Python for a previous internship and learned HTML and CSS to code this website in 3 days (just for fun). I learned how to kickbox, got over my fear of heights through rock climbing, and taught myself how to cook, work, and live on my own in a new city. I believe that people are capable of doing anything; the barrier is ourselves.

Creative. When I am not busy, I am drawing on any drawable surface, taking photographs, reading science fiction, exploring local art and history museums, or just lying on my bed, daydreaming. Art and storytelling celebrate the human experience, transcend time and space, and include relics of the past, tokens of the now, and predictors of the future. It inspires us to understand other lives and live better lives.

Team-Oriented. I thrive with teamwork and group activities. There is an incredible potential for mutual growth and learning within teams that I really value. Plus, it's more fun when you're working with friends!
